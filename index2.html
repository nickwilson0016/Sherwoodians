Welcome one and all to the first annual Sherwoodian Awards

The Woody’s if you will. Thank you to everyone who voted in this year's awards and congratulations to everyone who was nominated. The first award of the night is Fantasy Rookie of the Year. There were a lot of great options to choose from this season, but there were 4 that really stuck out. The nominees are:

Jaxon Dart - New York Giants QB
Dart came on to the scene fast this season. He finished as QB16 on the year and led all rookies in concussions sustained throughout the year.

Ashton Jeanty - Las Vegas Raiders RB
Jeanty started the season off slow and played for the dogshit Raiders. However, that couldn’t stop Jeanty from leading all rookies in fantasy points this year.

Emeka Egbuka - Tampa Bay Bucs WR
Egbuka had the biggest burst on to the scene with his week 5 breakout. However, the end of the season was mediocre at best.

TreVeyon Henderson - New England Patriots RB
Oh baby, did things start slow for Henderson. Barley played in games to start the season. But then holy shit. He's the fastest kid alive. Leading all rookies in 50+ yard rushes and 50+ yard touchdown runs.

The winner of the 2025Woody for Rookie of the Year goes to:
TreVeyon Henderson - This is TreVeyon's first nomination for Rookie of the Year and first win. TreVeyon was unanimously voted to win Rookie of the Year.
